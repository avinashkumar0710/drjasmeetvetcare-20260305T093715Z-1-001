Welcome to VetCare

One Roof, Many Services

Vetcare is a fully equipped veterinary clinic in Chhattisgarh & Odisha that accepts all emergency and regular medical, surgical, and dental cases. Our director Dr. Jasmeet Singh has 10 years of experience treating serious illnesses and providing the best wellness treatment for your pets.

We are dedicated to delivering excellent veterinary healthcare services, supported by outstanding facilities, cutting-edge technology, and a wonderful team of experienced veterinarians. We aim to provide every parent with a healthy and happy companion pet raised in a loving environment.
