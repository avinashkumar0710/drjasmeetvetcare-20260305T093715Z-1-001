Biography

A Lifetime Dedicated to Animal Care

Dr. Jasmeet Singh is our Managing Director — a renowned Veterinarian with a vast experience of over 10 years in the field of Veterinary Surgery, having worked as Consultant Surgeon at Dog Care Centre, Bhubaneswar and Allahabad since 2007.

Born on March 28, 1983, at R.S. Pura near the Indo-Pak border in Jammu & Kashmir, Dr. Jasmeet completed his secondary education in his native town and went on to obtain B.V.Sc. & AH — 2007 from OUAT, Bhubaneswar, followed by M.V.Sc. (Surgery) — 2009.

In 2012, he completed his Ph.D. and received the Gold Medal from the Indian Veterinary Research Institute, Bareilly. The main area of his research has been anesthesia and pain relief in animals, including the development of electroacupuncture therapy for rehabilitation of companion animals — for which he received the 2010 Gold Medal from the UP Chapter of the Indian Society for Veterinary Surgery.
